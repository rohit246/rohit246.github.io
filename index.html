BIO

ABOUT ME

I am PhD Student at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, under the supervision of Dr. Sandip Chakraborty and Dr. Bivas Mitra. My primary area of interest is in the field of Mobile Computing.
Prior to joining PhD, I was working as a Software Developer with Schneider Electric India Pvt. Ltd for two years (2013-2015). I then joined a Junior Research Fellow at IIT Kharagpur in 2015 and then joined as a PhD student in January 2016. I am a recipient of the TCS Research Fellowship.
